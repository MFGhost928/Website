    Introduction

    We invite you to come and stay in our self-contained quality 4 star motel. If you need bed and breakfast, simply order a continental breakfast from our breakfast menu.

    All our Nelson motel units / apartments have ensuite bathrooms and comfortable king sized beds that are easily remade into single beds if you would prefer this option.

    You have a choice of family, self catering motel apartments including:

    * Studio motel units

    * One bedroom motel units

    * Two bedroom motel units

    * Interlocking motel units for families, sports teams and large groups

    Our units are all fully double glazed, well insulated and equipped with thermostatically controlled quiet heaters.

    There really is everything you need for a relaxing and comfortable stay in the Nelson region.

    Arrow Motel Nelson are proud to be members of the Host Accommodation Group.

    We look forward to seeing you soon.
